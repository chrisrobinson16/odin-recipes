BBQ Corn

BBQ Corn:

This is the best Corn recipe you'll ever have.

With this recipe you will wow your family with the delicious flavors of this BBQ Corn.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Corn
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Mexican Crema

Steps for BBQ Corn!!

  1. Preheat the grill to medium high: that's 375 to 450 degrees.
  2. Husk the corn or tie back the leaves to make a handle
  3. Grill the cobs directly on the oven grates for 12 to 15 minutes, turning until blackened on all sides.
  4. Coat the corn with all the Ingredients.
  5. Enjoy!
